ALBERTO COUDEL

I am a self-taught sculptor, and my creative process always begins with the raw material. Born in Coimbra, Portugal, and having spent my formative years in Angola, I now live and work in the Algarve—where I find constant inspiration for my art.

I feel deeply privileged to live and create along this beautiful coastline. Each sculpture originates from a carefully selected stone or rock that I believe already contains the figure within it. My role is simply to reveal it. Some of the stones I use come from the Alentejo, but most are found along the beaches of the Algarve.

At times, a specific rock will determine the subject of the sculpture, guiding me as I work with nature to bring out the form and expression hidden inside. My process is instinctive and intuitive, and my love for the human form is present throughout all my works.
